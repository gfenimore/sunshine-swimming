Pool temperature matters!
For the safety and comfort of young swimmers, I require a heated pool (85°F+). Cold water makes it really hard for little ones to learn — and it's not safe for infants and toddlers.

If you're able to heat your pool or have access to a heated pool nearby, I'd love to hear from you! Feel free to reach out directly.
